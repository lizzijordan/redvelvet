    • Red Velvet is the newest girl group from Korean record label SM Entertainment. The group consists of five members: Irene, Yeri, Selungi, Joy, and Wendy. They debuted on August 2014 with the song "Happiess". Since their debut they have recorded a digital single, and two mini-album. Red Velvet does not have an official fanclub name yet, but the group seems to have big plans for the future.
